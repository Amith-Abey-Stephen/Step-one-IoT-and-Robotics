About StepOne

Are you a newbie to the world of electronics, IOT and embedded system. Are you curious to learn prototypes and DIY projects. Do you often need a kickstart a career in IOT and Arduino. Then this event is completely for you. When a kid wants to study electronics what should we do? Give them some LED’s and motors not a thick book of theory of electronics. This is the first step towards the world of microcontrollers and creating really cool stuffs in IOT. The event help you to delve into the nuances of IOT and embedded system.
